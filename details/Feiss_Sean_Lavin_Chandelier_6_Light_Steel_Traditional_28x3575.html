Feiss Sean Lavin 6-light Steel Chandelier

Elevate your space with this elegant and versatile Feiss chandelier that beautifully combines traditional style with a modern vintage aesthetic. Designed for both form and function, it's the perfect lighting solution for any room in your home.

Stylish Design for Timeless Appeal

This steel chandelier offers a warm, inviting look with its distressed white wood finish and French washed oak detailing. The 6-light design ensures even illumination, making it an ideal choice for dining rooms, entryways, and living areas. Its vintage chandelier appearance adds a unique touch to your decor, blending classic charm with contemporary sophistication.

Adaptable for Any Ceiling Type

Featuring an adjustable 15 feet wire and 5 feet chain, this chandelier is designed to fit seamlessly into homes with flat, sloped, vaulted, or cathedral ceilings. The flexibility in hanging height allows you to tailor the lighting to your decor and space.

Durable and Safe for Humid Environments

Whether you're installing it in your kitchen or a covered patio, this steel chandelier is built to last. Its damp rating ensures it remains safe and functional in humid conditions, making it a reliable addition to your home lighting system.

Comes with B10 Torpedo Candelabra 60-watt incandescent bulbs for a warm and cozy glow. The lighting is ideal for creating a welcoming atmosphere, whether you're hosting guests or enjoying quiet moments at home.
