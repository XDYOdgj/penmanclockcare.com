The GROHE Rainshower 150 SmartActive Hand Shower

Enhance your daily shower experience with the GROHE Rainshower 150 SmartActive Hand Shower. This advanced showerhead offers three jet types that let you enjoy a calming rainfall effect, a focused stream for targeted cleansing, and a strong jet for deep scrubbing. Whether you're relaxing or rushing, the SmartActive system adjusts automatically to provide the ideal water flow for your needs.

The sleek, scratch-resistant chrome finish adds a modern touch to any bathroom, and remains looking pristine even with regular use. Designed for long-term performance, the SpeedClean anti-limescale system minimizes buildup, ensuring your showerhead stays clean and efficient. Its DripStop technology prevents water from dripping during use, making it more convenient and user-friendly.

With a compact 150 mm design, the showerhead is easy to handle and store. Included care instructions and mounting accessories make installation and maintenance a breeze, while offering flexibility for your existing plumbing setup. Finally, rest assured with the 5-year manufacturer's warranty that guarantees quality and reliability for years to come.
