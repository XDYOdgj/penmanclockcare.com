Elegant, Safe, and Stylish LED Candles

Bring a soft, warm glow to your space with these flameless LED candles from EZiKitchen. Perfect for weddings, parties, and seasonal party decorations, they offer a safe alternative to traditional candles without the risk of open flame or melting wax.

Each candle is equipped with a built-in 6-hour timer, allowing for effortless automatic operation. After 6 hours, the lights turn off for 18 hours and then repeat, making it ideal for long events or continuous use. Whether you're hosting a festive gathering or creating a cozy atmosphere at home, these lamps provide a reliable and charming lighting solution.

Crafted with waterproof construction, they are ready for use in any weather condition, both indoors and outdoors. Available in 2x4 inch sizes, they fit seamlessly into pillar candle displays, mantels, and other decorative arrangements. The sleek, modern design of the slim and tall pillar candle options makes them easy to place and versatile for any setting.

These battery operated candles are child-friendly and completely safe for households with pets or young children. Ideal for anniversaries, housewarmings, and special occasions, they are a perfect addition to any celebration or aesthetic.
